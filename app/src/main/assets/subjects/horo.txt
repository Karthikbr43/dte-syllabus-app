UNIT I: THEORY OF TIME MEASUREMENT
Introduction to time measurement, Earth rotation, Sidereal day, Solar & mean solar days, Unit of time, Latitudes and National standard times calendar
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: TIME  MEASUREMENT IN OLDEN DAYS
Electric candle, Burning lenity card, Water bowl, Sun dial, Hour glass.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: GENERAL WORKING PRINCIPLE AND MECHANICS OF SIMPLE STRIKING SYSTEMS OF CLOCKS
Simple pendulum, Compound pendulum, Compressing pendulum, Motive power, Train of wheels, Indicating system, Frames main springs, Escapement, A.C drives, Battery drive, Electronic drive and Rack 2 locking plate types.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: GENERAL WORKING PRINCIPLE OF TIME PIECE
Alarm/repeater Mechanism, Train of wheels, Trail train, Regulation, Function of complete balance wheels  and Pivots bearings.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT V: MECHANISM OF WATCHES
Plates, bridges, Motive power to transmission, Escapement balance & hair spring, Regulation, winding, Hand setting mechanisms, train of wheels casing, Trail train, Wheels 2 jewel bearing. Resolve power, Pulse system, A.C.drives, Transistor clock, Electronic drive, Train of wheels, Gear train. Self winding principle, Self winding gear train, Drive of Calendar mechanisms, Jumpers & types of correction systems.
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [45 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: SOLUTIONS & METHODS USED IN CLEANING AND LUBRICANTS
Different cleaning solutions used in horology, Methods of cleaning. Epilame treatment, Methods & types of lubricants used.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>
