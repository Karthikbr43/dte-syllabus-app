UNIT - I: Introduction to Semiconductor Devices
<b>Semiconductor Physics:</b> Atomic structure, Neil Bohr's atomic theory, definition of conductors, insulators and semiconductors, energy level diagrams. <b>Semiconductors:</b> Classification and types, intrinsic and extrinsic, P-type and N-type semiconductors, majority and minority carriers, recombination, effect of temperature.<b>PN junction:</b> Formation, depletion region, barrier potential, reverse breakdowns, PN junction as diode, symbol, biasing modes, V-I characteristics, reverse saturation current, diode current equation, effect of temperature on diode current, ideal diode, basic diode ratings. <b>Zener diode:</b> Symbol, Principle, Zener breakdown, V-I characteristics. <b>Application of Diodes:</b> diode as a switch, voltage regulator.
<br><b>[11 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - II: Bipolar Transistors
<b>Transistor Basics:</b> Definition, formation of transistor-PNP and NPN, symbols, working principle, transistor current equation. <b>Modes of operation:</b> CB, CE and CC Configuration modes, input and output characteristics in CB and CE configuration, definition of Riand Ro, α, β, and γ relation between them, simple problems, comparison of CB, CE and CC modes. <b>Applications:</b> switch and amplifier in CE configuration, thermal runaway, role of heat sinks.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT – III: Field Effect Transistor
<b>FET basics:</b> Physical structure of FET, principle of operation, P-channel and N-channel, symbols, drain and transfer characteristics, definition of pinch-off voltage, rd, gm, µ and their relationship, comparison of JFET and BJT, Applications of JFET. <b>MOSFET:</b> Types-Depletion and enhancement, P-channel and N-channel, physical structure, comparison of MOSFET and JFET.
<br><b>CMOS:</b> Working principle, low-power consumption feature, limitations, inverter, Applications of CMOS, comparison with MOSFET.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - IV: Special Semiconductor Devices
Physical structure, Working principle, characteristic curves, symbol and Applications of following semiconductor devices: UJT, SCR, DIAC and TRIAC. Features of varactor, tunnel diode, Gunn diode, PIN diode, and Schottky diode.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - V: Fabrication of Integrated ICs
Definition and need of IC’s, 	advantages and disadvantages, classification of ICs based on structure, scale of integration, function. Fabrication process of monolithic ICs. Fabrication of diode and capacitor.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT - VI: Opto-semiconductor Devices
<b>Definitions:</b> photo emission, photoconduction, photovoltaic effect with Application examples. <b>Features:</b> photodiode, phototransistor, LED, Opto-coupler, LED lamps, solar cell, solar panel, LASER and MASER. 
<br><b>[07 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>
