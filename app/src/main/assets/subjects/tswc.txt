UNIT I:LAND USE PLANNING
Introduction, Land use capability, Classification of land suited for agriculture, Land suited for permanent vegetation, Land capability sub classes, Crop classification based on soil conversation value.
<br><b>[04 Hrs] [10 Marks] </b>

UNIT II:CONTOURING
Introduction, Contour farming,  Strip cropping, field strip and butter strip cropping, Strip cropping , Crop rotation,  Conservation tillage,  Contour trenching, Vetiver grass for soil and water conservation and land rehabilitation.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT III:CONTOUR BUNDING
Indian experience in Bunding, Design and construction of contour bunds, Design criteria for bunds, Construction of bund with buck scraper, Bench terraces with stone walls, Waste weirs, and Simple problems on graded bund.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV:TERRACING
Introduction, Types of terraces, Terrace design spacing,  Terrace grades, terrace length, Terrace cross section, Ran off terraced areas,  Terrace channel capacity, Planning the terrace system, Selection of outlets,  Terrace locations and layout,  Simple problems an graded terrace.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT V:VEGETATED OUTLETS AND WATER COURSES
Introduction, Design of vegetated water ways,  Establishment of vegetation and water way maintenance,    Grass land management, Forestay in soil  conservation, Management of forests, Agro forestry,  Silva pastoral     system, Simple problems on design of grassed water way
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI:GULLY CONTROL AND RAVINE RECLAMATION
Important definitions, Extent and causes of gully erosion and ravines, Land capability  classification     for ravine land reclamation, Planning gully control , Controlling run-off,  Vegetation in gully control,  Gully control procedures, Gully control structures temporary check dams, Woven wire dams, Brush dams,  Loose rocks dams, Permanent gully control     structures
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>
