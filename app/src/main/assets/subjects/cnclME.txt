<b>Experiments:</b>

<br><br><b>PART - A: CNC turning centre part programming</b>

<br><br>1.	Develop  a part program for step turning and simulate
<br>2.	Develop a part program for taper turning and simulate
<br>3.	Develop a part program for circular interpolation and simulate 
<br>4.	Develop a part program for multiple turning operation and simulate 
<br>5.	Develop a part program for thread cutting, grooving and simulate
<br>6.	Develop a part program for internal drills, boring and simulate

<br><br><b>PART - B: CNC machining centre part programming</b>

<br><br>7.	Develop a part program for grooving and simulate on CNC Milling
<br>8.	Develop a part program for drilling (canned cycle) and simulate 
<br>9.	Develop a part program for mirroring with subroutines and simulate 
<br>10.	Develop a part program for rectangular and circular pocketing and simulate

<br><br><b>Note:</b>
<br>Students would: 
<br>a. Sketch each part with dimensions. 
<br>b. Prepare CNC part programme using G and M codes with ISO format. 
<br>C. Show various zeros and tool path on part sketch with color codes and dimensions. 
<br>d. Simulate the prepared part programme using available simulation software’s. 
<br>e. Prepare the parts on CNC
