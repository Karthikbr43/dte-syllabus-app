Unit IV: Individual Assignments and Life skills
The students will perform ANY ONE of the following activities individually (other similar activities may be considered) in both the sections

<br><br><b>A. Individual assignments</b>
<br><br>1. Collecting Failure data for automobile / machines / equipment.
<br>2. Study of Hydraulic system for any one application like – dumpers, Earth moving equipment, Auto service station.
<br>3. Survey of oils used for hydraulic circuits – specifications, properties, costs, manufacturers names etc.
<br>4. Study any one type of CNC machining centre and prepare report on tooling and tool holding devices
<br>5. For a given job write a sequence of operations performed by automated manufacturing system. Draw a block diagram of control system to perform above operations
<br>6. Survey of types of bearings involving information about construction working principles, mounting, lubrication, materials, advantages, limitations and cost.
<br>7. Prepare a trouble shooting chart for any refrigeration system and suggest remedial measures to avoid failures
<br>8. For a drilling or milling operations on a simple machine component,
<br>9. Draw a jig or fixtures showing various features like locating clamping, fool proofing etc.
<br>10. Compare non traditional methods on the basis of working principles, accuracy , MRR, Applications and limitations a) EBM b) PAM C)AJM d)WJM

<br><br><b>B. Life skills</b>
<br><br>1.	Conduct aptitude, general knowledge test, IQ test, Solve Puzzles, quizzes etc.
<br>2.	Set the goal for personal development
<br>3.	Develop good habits to overcome stress

<br><br><b>Methodology for conducting activity:</b>
<br><br>1.   The teacher will assign a topic for individual student; give sufficient time to complete the task. Ask the student to submit a hand written report
<br>2.   The teacher  should  conduct  any one  specified  life  skill  activity  with  local  NGO/ placement cell/ISTE student chapter/CCTEK/  NSS unit of the institute. The student should present his/her experiences in a class and make report.
