<br><br><b>Student Activity</b>

<br><br>1.	Student should prepare a decorative clay model and fire the same. 
<br><br>2.	Student should prepare a casting slip by keeping density of 1.65gm/cc and cast a hollow ware.
<br><br>3.	Prepare Plaster of Paris model by carving.
<br><br>4.	Prepare a fired article of your own body composition and make a report on it.
<br><br>5.	Student should surf the internet and make a report on two shaping methods and their features.
<br><br>6.	Student should surf the internet and make a report on advanced casting methods.
<br><br>7.	Student should prepare a dry pressed article of their own composition, fire the same and make report on it.
<br><br>8.	Student should study the viscosity behaviour with varying percentage of defloculant keeping the water constant. And make a report on the same.

<br><br><b>Note:</b> Student should prepare hand written report with minimum 2-3 pages and reports should not be copies from earlier batches. And other similar tasks can be assigned.
