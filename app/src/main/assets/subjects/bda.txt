UNIT I: Data in connected world
The Liberated data, Caged data, Big data is Big news, FERED, Search based Internet data, Survey data.Introduction to Big Data .Characteristics of Data.Evolution of big Data. Definition of Big data. Challenges with Big Data. What and why  is Big Data. Traditional Business Intelligence versus Big Data. A Typical Data WarehouseEnvironment .What is changing in Realms of big Data?
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [26 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: Big Data Analytics
Big Data Analytics. Classification of Analytics. Greatest Challenges that prevent businesses from capitalizing of Big Data. Importance of Big Data .Data science. Data scientist. Terminologies used in big Data Environments.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [22 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: Introduction to Hadoop
Introduction to Hadoop. Distributed Computing Challenges. Hadoop -History, Overview, Use case Hadoop Distributors. Processing data with Hadoop.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [16 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: Introduction to MongoDB
Introduction to MongoDB terms used in RDBMS MongoDB. Data Types .Query language in Mongo.
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [33 Marks] </b>

UNIT V: Introduction to R Programming
Introduction to R Programming, R environment, Single –mode data structures,multi-mode data structures.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [24 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: Working with R Programming
Writing functions , loops and summaries.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [24 Marks] </b>
