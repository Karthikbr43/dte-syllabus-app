<br><br><b>Suggested Student Activity:</b>

<br><br>1. Visit nearby electronic/electrical repair shops collect different transistors and ICs and make a report on them containing photos, pin configuration and applications.
<br>2. Visit nearby computer repair shop/ automobile service stations/websites and collect information on different ICs, components used in general computer or ECU and make a report with photographs/block diagrams/circuits, working and applications.
<br>3. Visit nearby two wheel/four wheeler service stations/garages/websites and make report of different automotive sensors and actuators and components seen with specification, materials used and manufacturing processes used along with photographs.
<br>4. Visit nearby two wheel/four wheeler service stations/garages and collect different electrical/electronic control circuits of vehicles.
<br>5. Visit nearby four wheeler service stations/ websites and collect information on checking and troubleshooting procedures of different automobile sensors and actuators.
<br>6. Visit nearby four wheeler service stations/ websites and collect information on different OBDII standard trouble codes.

<br><br><b>Note:</b> 
<br>1. Student should prepare a hand written report on any one of the above/similar activity, which helps in achieving above course outcomes.
<br>2. The report prepared should be approved by the concerned staff and HOD.
<br>3. The activity group should consist of maximum of three students.
