Unit I:	Carpentry Practice
Study of the joints in roofs, doors, windows and furniture available in Polytechnic

<br><br><b>Hands-on-exercise</b>:
<br>1. Demonstration of different wood working machines/ Power Tools of different wood working process, like planning, marking, Chiseling, Grooving, turning of wood etc.
<br>2. Exercise on One simple Wood work joints like Mortise and tennon, dovetail joint by sawing, planning and cutting.
<br>3. Measure quantity of wood required for a given task.

Unit II: Fitting Shop

<b>Hands-on-exercise</b>
<br>1. Demonstration of different fitting tools and drilling machines and power tool
<br>2. Demonstration of different operations like chipping, filing, drilling, tapping, cutting etc.
<br>3. Exercise on One simple fitting job involving practice of chipping, filing, drilling, tapping, cutting etc.
<br>4.	Making of male and female gauges with V joint, rectangular joint and semi circular joint
<br>5.	Prepare utility articles like screw drivers, hinges, hexagonal nut.
<br>6.	Measure quantity of metal required for a given task
