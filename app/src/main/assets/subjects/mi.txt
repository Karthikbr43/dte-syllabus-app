UNIT I: LINEAR MEASUREMENTS
Introduction: Steel Rule,Calipers,SurfacePlate,AnglePlate,V-Blocks,StraightEdges,Spirit Level, Combination Set, Universal Surface Gauge, Engineers Square, Engineers Parallels, Radius Gauge, Feeler Gauge, Angle Gauges, Pitch Screw Gauges, Vernier Calipers, Vernier Height Gauge, Micrometer-Outside and Inside Micrometer, Thread Micrometer, Slip Gauges. Straight Edges.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: STANDARDS OF MEASUREMENTS AND COMPARATORS
Introduction: Standards, Line Standard-Yard and Metre, Sub-divisions of Standards, End Standard, Wavelength Standard, Relation of End Standard and Line Standard to International Standard, Measurement and Calibration System, Need and Function of Inspection, Calibration System requirements. Comparators:Characteristics,Uses, Advantages and Disadvantages of various comparators, Mechanical Comparator -Reed Type and Sigma Comparators, Mechanical Optical Comparator, Electrical and Electronics Comparators, Pneumatic Comparator. 
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: LIMITS, FITS, TOLERANCES AND GAUGES
Introduction, Tolerance, Interchangeability and Selective Assembly, Terminology for Limits, Fitsand Tolerances, Types of Fits, Holes and Shafts Basis System,Plain Gauges, Types of Gauges-Plug Gauge, Ring Gauge, Snap Gauges, Positional tolerances, Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerance, Geometric Characteristics and Symbols.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: ANGULAR MEASUREMENT AND CIRCULAR DIVISION
Introduction, Vernier and Optical Bevel Protractor, Sine Bar-Principle, Uses, Limitations and Precaution, Sine Centre, Angle Gauges-Uses, Spirit Level-Characteristics, Clinometers, Autocollimeter, Angle Dekkor-Uses.  Circular Division-Rotary Table, Dividing Heads and Circular Tables.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT V: MEASUREMENT OF SURFACE FINISH AND METROLOGY OF SCREW THREAD
Introduction, Meaning of Surface Structure, Surface Roughness, Terminology, Methods of Measuring Surface Finish, Profilometer, Tomlinsons Surface Meter, Taylor-Hobson Talysurf. Screw Thread Terminology, Effect of Pitch Errors, Measurement of Various Elements of Thread. Two wire andThree wire Method, Best size wire.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: DIAL INDICATORS AND INDICATING GAUGES
Introduction,Requirements of Good Dial Indicators, Advantages of Dial Indicators, Classifications, Working Mechanism of Dial Indicators, Range, Description of Various Parts, General Care and Use of Dial gauges, Accuracy of Dial Indicators, Guide Lines in the Use of Dial Indicators to Reduce the Effect of Calibration Errors, Dial Indicators Components, Accessories and Attachments, Dial Gauges as Per  Indian Standards, Application of Dial Gauges . 
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>
