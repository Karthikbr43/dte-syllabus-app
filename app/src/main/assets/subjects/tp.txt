UNIT - I: Introduction
Objects of town planning, principles of town planning, Origin and growth of towns – development of towns, Modern town planning in India, Socio – Economic aspects of town planning. Selection of site for an ideal town.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT - II: Surveys & Planning and Zoning
<b>Surveys & Planning:</b> Various types of surveys to be conducted for town planning project. Data’s to be collected in different types of town planning survey. Types of planning, -a brief note on urban, rural and regional planning
<br><b>Zoning:</b> Definition – objects and principles of zoning. Advantages of zoning, Special Economic Zone (SEZ), Maps for zoning.
<br><br><b>[12 Hrs] [35 Marks] </b>

UNIT - III: Housing, Slums, Public buildings & Industries
<b>Housing:</b> Classification of residential building as per HUDCO norms, Housing in villages, Low Cost Housing, Housing policy, different types of housing agencies involved in housing, investment in Housing, Housing Problems in India
<br><b>Slums:</b> Causes, growth, characteristics, effects, slum clearance and re-housing, prevention of slum formation, financial assistance for slum clearance.
<br><b>Public buildings & Industries:</b> Classification – location, Design Principles of public building, Grouping of public buildings. Effects of Industries on towns and cities, classification of industries, regulation of their location.
<br><br><b>[09 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT - IV: Recreation measures and Master Plan
<b>Recreation measures:</b> Parks- park ways, Playgrounds, Theme parks, boulevards and their space standards, knowledge of Landscape sketches for a) Residential Building, b) Public Buildings and c) Industrial Buildings.
<br><b>Master Plan:</b> Meaning – Definition – objects and necessity of master plan, Data and Drawings required for master planning. Preparation of a layout plan for a residential area showing LIG, MIG and HIG houses and other amenities (not to scale).
<br><br><b>[09 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT - V: Re-planning Existing Towns
General - Objects of re-planning – Analyzing the defects of existing towns -–difficulties in Master Planning of existing towns / cities - Urban renewal projects, merging of suburban areas – Decentralization - Satellite Towns – Smart cities- definition and features. 
<br><br><b>[07 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - VI: Urban Roads and Traffic Management
<b>Urban Roads:</b> objects, requirements, classification, types of street systems, through and bypass roads, outer and inner ring roads, expressways, freeways.
<br><b>Traffic Management:</b> objects, traffic surveys, traffic congestion, traffic control, road junctions and intersections, parking, road accidents, traffic capacity of roads, traffic islands, roundabouts, traffic signals, road signs, road markings, street lighting in a town.
<br><br><b>[09 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

<br>18.	Prepare a report on the Smart City Concept.

<br>20.	Study the different modern slum improvement techniques and prepare a report.
