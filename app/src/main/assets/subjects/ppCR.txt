Unit IV: Individual Assignments and Life skills
The students will perform ANY ONE of the following activities individually (other similar activities may be considered) in both the sections

<br><br><b>A. Individual assignments</b>
<br><br>1.	Collecting Failure data for glass batch / ceramic equipment.
<br>2.	Study of elevator system for any one application like – raw material transferring, batch conveying etc.
<br>3.	Survey of ceramic raw materials used for different industries– specifications, properties, costs of materials etc.
<br>4.	Study any one type of ceramic process like glaze making, refractory shape pressing, tile making process etc. and prepare report on that process
<br>5.	Survey of types of refractories involving properties relating to working condition, advantages, limitations and cost
<br>6.	Prepare a chart of working procedure of any ceramic industry devices/equipments like furnace, kiln, batch charger, burner cleaning, regenerator cleaning etc.
<br>7.	Draw a Cement/Glass  plant layout showing different subunits and process
<br>8.	Compare manual operation and automatic operation of furnaces, charger, conveyor, firing with reversal etc
<br>9.	Make a report on ‘combustion air’ for firing in various ceramic industry
<br>10.	Study the different techniques to reduce heat loss in various ceramic industries
