UNIT I:INTRODUCTION AND PROCESS OF FIRE
<b>INTRODUCTION:</b> Fire in history, fire losses, major fires in India, fire safety through regulation, fire causes, interested parties in fire safety, fire protection standards and practices, how much safety is enough 
<br><b>PROCESS OF FIRE:</b> Introduction, fire triangle, fire tetrahedron, fuels, smouldering, metal fires, combustion of dusts, ease of ignition, heat, ignition process, fire stages, structure of flames, Flame height, fire plume, fire load and materials characteristics, development and growth of fire, fire spread in buildings, Heat transfer related to fire 
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: FIRE SCIENCE FOR THE BUILT ENVIRONMENT 
Introduction, building loads, life safety and property protection, materials of constructions, fire properties of materials, fire testing, testing for resistance to fire, Standard time- temperature curves, other fire tests, passive fire protection, segregation and separation, compartmentation, Escape from the buildings, products of fire, fire spread in buildings, toxic properties of fire products. 
<br><b>[09 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: FIRE SAFETY IN BUILDINGS 
Building types and fire safety related peculiarities, objectives of fire safety, elements of fire safety design, fire safety concept tree, roles of professionals and regulations, fire safety for disabled people, design elements and safety measures, fire safety in historic buildings. 
<br><b>[09 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: FIRE DETECTION AND ALARMS 
Introduction, basics of detectors and alarms, detector types based on effects, heat detectors, smoke detectors, optical flame detectors, gas sensing detectors, alarm systems, fire alarm systems and control panel, principle of operation, selecting detectors and alarm systems. 
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT V: FIRE EXTINGUISHERS 
Fire fighting fundamentals, first-aid fire fighting extinguishers, common features of extinguishers, types of extinguishers, classification of fires and suitability of extinguishers, rating extinguishers, hazard categorisation and placement of extinguishers.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: FIXED FIRE PROTECTION SYSTEMS 
Introduction, water based fixed fire protection systems, automatic sprinklers, foam systems, water spray systems, water mist systems, carbon dioxide flooding systems, clean agents, draining out fire water, introduction to risk management and fire insurance.
