UNIT I: CONVENTIONAL REPRESENTATION
Standard convention using SP–46 (1988), Materials like Cast Iron, Mild Steel, Brass, Bronze, Aluminum, wood, Glass, Concrete and Rubber. Long and short break in pipe, rod and shaft. Various sections- Half, removed,-Standard convention of Knurling, Splined shafts, and chain wheels-Springs with square and flat ends, Gears, sprocket wheel-Counter sunk & Counter bore.
<br><b>[09 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: DIALS
Pressure gauge dial, Dial indicators, Tachometer, Ammeter, Voltmeter and Speedometer
<br><b>[09 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: SECTION OF INSTRUMENTS
Sectional views, Cutting plane line, Type of sections -Full section, Half section, Top half in section, Bottom half in section, Right half in section, Left half in section, Partial or Broken section, Revolved section, Removed section, Offset section and Practice on sectioning of different types of instruments.
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: PRODUCTION DRAWINGS
Surface roughness, Indication of machining, Symbol showing direction of lay, Roughness grades, Machining allowances, Machining symbols used in industry.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT V: ASSEMBLY DRAWING OF INSTRUMENTS
Introduction to the assembly drawing, Steps involved in preparing assembly drawing, Details sequence in assembly, Preparation of details and Assembly of parts with sectional views of Water meter, Dial indicator, Pressure gauge, Wall clock, Parallel rule and Movement holder. (Front, Top and Side views).
<br><b>[36 Hrs] [70 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: TRACING AND BLUE PRINTING
Tracing of the above exercises and Blue printing the same.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [-- Marks] </b>

<br><br><b>Suggested Student Activity:</b>

<br><br>1. Select at least four simple measuring instruments. Get them approved by teacher. Measure and sketch them in a drawing sheet with dimensions.
<br>2. Select at least one simple measuring device, disassemble it in group of 5-6 students, Get them approved by teacher. Measure and sketch them in a drawing sheet with dimensions. Example- assembly of Water meter, Dial indicator etc.
<br>3. Bring Actual assembly of measuring instrument from workshop/industry, measure dimensions, sketch it and make 2D production drawing for the same.
