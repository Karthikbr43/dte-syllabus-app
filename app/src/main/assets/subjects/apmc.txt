UNIT - I: AIR POLLUTION AND METEROLOGY
<b>Introduction:</b> Air pollution definition, importance & composition of atmosphere, Sources and classification of air pollutants.
<br><b>Meteorology and Air pollution:</b> Meteorological factors influencing air pollution, Plume behaviour, Wind rose.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - II: EFFECTS OF AIR POLLUTION
<b>Factor affecting human health:</b> Mechanism of action of air pollutants, Health effects, Effect of specific pollutant on human, Effect of specific pollutant on animals
<br><b>Effect of air pollutant on plants:</b> Structure of normal leaf, Air pollutants affecting plants, Forms of damage to leaves & injury to plants, Effect of specific air pollutants on plants
<br><b>Economic effect of air pollution:</b> Mechanism of deterioration of materials by air pollution, Material damage and economical loss due to air pollution, Effect of air pollution on Taj Mahal
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - III: AIR POLLUTION SAMPLING
<b>Sampling procedures:</b> Basic consideration for air sampling, Air sampling devices for particulates and gases, Duration and location of sampling kit, List the Sampling methods..
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - IV: ANYLSIS OF AIR SAMPLES
<b>Analysis of samples:</b> Factors affecting accurate data, Chemical methods, Instrumental methods. Absorption spectrometric methods, Thermal conductctivity 
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT - V: CONTROL EQUIPMENTS
<b>Control of air pollution:</b> Objectives of using control equipments Settling chambers, Cyclone, Bag Filters, Electrostatic precipitator, Scrubber, Factors affecting choice of equipment 
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [35 Marks] </b>

UNIT - VI: AIR POLLUTION IN INDUSTRIES
<b>Air Pollution from major industrial operations:</b> Cement and Thermal power plants.
<br><b>Smoke pollution:</b> sources and effects of smoke.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>
