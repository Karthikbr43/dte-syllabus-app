UNIT-I: Production Management
Management- Functions of Management, Henry-Fayol’s principles of management, Functions of Manager,
<br>Organization- types of organization 
<br>Production planning and Control (PPC) - Functions of PPC: planning, routing, scheduling,  
<br>dispatching and Inspection ,
<br>Productivity: factors to improve productivity
<br>Project Management - Market survey, Project planning, Project capacity, selection of site for project, Aids of project management-CPM (Critical path Method)- PERT (Program evaluation and review Technique).
<br>Cost analysis - Objectives of costing, Elements of costing, Material cost, Labor cost & Overheads,
<br><br><b>[08 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT-II: Material Management And Maintenance
Material management- different methods of purchasing, Purchase procedure, Comparative statement, purchase order, Tender-Types of tender
<br>Inventory and Inventory control - Definition, Functions of inventory control, Steps involved in inventory control, ABC Analysis, Advantages of Inventory Control,
<br>Management Information System (MIS) - Objectives of MIS, 
<br>Material Requirement Planning (MRP)-concept, applications. 
<br>Enterprise resource planning (ERP) - concept, features and applications.
<br>Plant maintenance -Types of maintenance, Preventive maintenance, Break down Maintenance, Advantages and disadvantages, Total Productive Maintenance, benefits of TPM, Tools of TPM, planned maintenance and predictive maintenance.
<br><br><b>[08 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT-III: Total Quality Management
Quality Control - Factors affecting quality, Advantages of quality Control, Inspection, types of inspection,
<br>Total Quality Management -Principles of Total Quality Management, PDSA cycles, PDCA cycles, Quality Circles, 
<br>Statistical Quality Control (SQC), - Acceptance sampling, Sampling inspection, 
<br>TQM Tools- Control Charts, Histograms, Pareto Charts, Cause and effect diagram 
<br>SIX SIGMA–Concepts, Benefits of Six Sigma, 
<br>Quality Certification Systems- ISO 9000 series, (ISO 9000, ISO 9001, ISO9002, ISO9003 & ISO 9004,) ISO9000 quality certification procedure, Benefits of ISO Certification
<br>ISO-14000, Benefits of ISO-14000 Certification.
<br><br><b>[10 Hrs] [45 Marks] </b>

UNIT-IV: Modern Production Management Techniques
Lean Manufacturing- Objectives, types of wastes, Steps for lean   Production, Benefits
<br>Just in Time-concepts and benefits, 
<br>KANBAN production- concepts and benefits, 
<br>Kaizen- concepts and benefits
<br>SAP (Systems Applications and Products) - concepts and benefits
<br>World class Manufacturing (WCM) - Basic concept-Principles of world class manufacturing
<br>Concurrent Engineering-Elements of Concurrent Engineering
<br><br><b>[08 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT-V: Industrial Relation And Labour Laws
Introduction to industrial relation -Objectives of industrial relation, International labour    
<br>Organization (ILO), Objectives of ILO,
<br>The Indian Factories Act-1948-Important definitions, Health provisions, Safety provisions,    
<br>Welfare provisions, working hour’s provisions.
<br>The Payment of Wages Act 1936-Payment of wages, Deductions from salary, Imposition of Fine.
<br>The Minimum Wages Act 1948- Objectives, Provisions.
<br>The Apprentices Act 1961-Provisions under apprentice act
<br>The Workmen’s Compensation Act 1923¬- Important definitions, Provisions, Employers liability for compensation.
<br>The Employees State Insurance Act 1948- Objectives, Benefits to employees 
<br>The Employees’ Provident Funds & Miscellaneous Provisions Act 1952- Salient features of the   Act. 
<br>The Payment of Gratuity Act 1972- Salient features of the act.
<br><br><b>[08 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT-VI: Occupational  Health and Safety
Introduction to Occupational Safety, National safety council of India, Indian standard on OH& S management system, occupational health & safety management system (OHSAS), OHSAS 18001, How to use OHSAS 18001.
<br>Accident-causes for accident, Direct and indirect losses due to an accident, Steps to prevent accidents, Personal protective devices for Mechanical hazards, Role of safety officer in industry
<br>Fire safety-Fire prevention and Protection, Sources of Fire, Principles of fire extinguishing,  A, B, C-Types of fire extinguishers.
<br>Electrical safety - Hazardous of Electricity, Safety precautions to prevent electrical shocks.
<br>Industrial Pollution- Air pollution, Water pollution, Noise pollution, Soil pollution, Sources, Effects and Prevention of above pollutions, Industrial wastes and their control.
<br>Environment - Climate change, Global warming, acid rain, Green house effect, ozone layer, depletion- Causes, effects and Preventive measures, National environmental Legislations
<br><br><b>[10 Hrs] [40 Marks] </b>
