<br><br>1. Prepare Check list for different items of following type of Civil Engineering works.
<br>a. Load Bearing Building Structure.
<br>b. Framed structure type of building
<br>c. W.B.M.Road
<br>d. Septic Tank
<br>e. Community well
<br><br>2. Writing the rules of deduction of openings for below mentioned items of work as per IS 1200.
<br>a. Brick / Stone masonry
<br>b. Plastering / Pointing
<br><br>3. Preparing detailed estimate of a RCC single & two storied existing residential building for all items of work.
<br><br>4. Prepare the lead statement for earth work excavation for a Road.
<br><br>5. Prepare the lead and lift statement for a building.
<br><br>6. Student should visit the site and study the no of labours required for a particular item of work and compare it with the SR by doing Rate analysis as per site observation.
<br><br>7. Collect the market data for cost of construction materials and implement in rate analysis and compare it with the SR book.
<br><br>8. Rate analysis to be done for construction activities by using alternate materials like M-sand for River sand and analyse the difference of rates.
<br><br>9. Rate analysis for works under Lump sum (LS) head to be studied in detail and compared with present SR.
<br><br>10. Detailed estimate of any building before project to be compared during execution and after completion of project.
<br><br>11. Reconciliation of materials for a particular item need to done for an ongoing project.
<br><br>12. Detailed estimate for any two or more residential buildings to be compared and rate per unit area to be find out which will help in present market survey.
<br><br>13. Visit any construction site and study weekly/monthly RA bill submission from Contractors.
<br><br>14. Collecting old set of tender document and writing a report on it.
<br><br>15. Collection of tender notices published in newspapers for various items of civil engineering works (at least 5) write salient features of them.
<br><br>16. Drafting a tender notice for construction of a civil engineering work ( W. B. M. Road, residential building )
<br><br>17. Preparation of tender document for the building (detailed estimate prepared for RCC. building in estimating and costing shall be used) of various account forms from PWD & writing report on it.
<br><br>18. Writing a report on store procedure and account producer of PWD for it (Guest lecture of PWD official may be arranged.)
<br><br>19. Writing detailed specifications for one item from each of following :
<br>a. Irrigation engineering system
<br>b. Transportation engineering system.
<br>c. Environment engineering system.
<br>d. Building construction system
