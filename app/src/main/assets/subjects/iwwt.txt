UNIT I: Need of Industrial Wastewater Treatment and Wastewater treatment principles
<b>Need of Industrial Wastewater Treatment</b> - Characteristics of industrial wastewater. Difference between industrial & domestic wastes. Methods of disposal of industrial wastewater. Effluent standards for stream disposal and land disposal. Self purification of streams.  
<br><b>Wastewater treatment principles</b> - Waste volume reduction, Strength reduction, Equalization and proportioning, Chemical method-Neutralization, Biological treatment. Removal of inorganic materials
