UNIT – I: EXTRACTION
Liquid–liquid extraction – Liquid equilibrium – Triangular charts and their use – Liquid system – Choice of solvent for extraction equipments for extraction – Co-current, cross–Current and counter-current extraction (principles only) description of liquid–liquid extraction equipments.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - II: LEACHING
Principles of leaching – Equipment for leaching – Tray thickener - Ballman extractor – Rotocel extractor. 
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - III: ADSORPTION
Definition – Principles - Physical adsorption – Chemisorption - Examples – Nature of adsorbents – Adsorption equilibrium – industrial adsorbers - Effect of temperature on adsorption operation. Pressure swing adsorption (PSA) – uses – types – Temperature swing adsorber – 2 bed TSA & 3 bed TSA 
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT – IV: ION EXCHANGE
Theory of ion exchange – Fundamental principles – Relative affinities of various ions – Ion exchange resins – Resin types – mass transfer resistance in ion exchange – Ion exchange operations – cycle – Mixed bed ion exchange – Applications of Ion exchange
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT – V:  ABSORPTION
Gas absorption principles – Equilibrium solubility of gases – Ideal and non ideal liquid solution – Henry’s law choice of solvent for absorption – Equilibrium curve and operating line for absorption and desorption system – Tray towers and packed towers for absorption – Absorption factor – Height equivalent of theoretical plate – Statement of equations for calculations of height of absorption tower.
<br>Packed towers construction and operation – Regulator and random packing – Characteristics of packing – Packing supports – Liquid distributor – Entrainment separator – Loading of packed towers.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT – VI: SEEPARATION TECHNIQUES
Novel separations (only basic principles and applications) – Super critical fluid extraction – Perevaporation – Membrane separation – Electrodialysis - Reserve Osmosis – Chromatographic column separation .
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>
