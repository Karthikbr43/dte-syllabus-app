UNIT I: FORCE MEASUREMENT
Introduction to Force Measurement, Basic methods of Force Measurement, Scales & Balances, Equal arm balance, Un-equal arm balance, Multiple lever systems, Pendulum scale, Accelerometers, Hydraulic & Pneumatic load cells, Elastic sensing elements, Proving rings.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: TORQUE MEASUREMENT
Introduction to Torque Measurement, Torque reaction methods, Strain gauge torsion method, Stroboscopic method, Optical torsion meter, Electrical torsion meter.
<br><b>[07 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: DYNAMOMETERS
Introduction to Shaft Power Measurement, Dynamometers - Classification, Rope brake dynamometer, Prony brake dynamometer, Hydraulic dynamometer, Eddy current Brake dynamometer, D.C dynamometers, A.C dynamometers - Servo controlled dynamometers, Instantaneous measurement of power.  
<br><b>[09 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: TRANSDUCERS & STRAIN GAUGES
Introduction to Transducers, Classification, Transducers sensitivity, Variable resistance transducers, Variable inductance transducers, LVDT, Capacitive transducers, Piezo-electric transducers, Wheat-stone bridge circuit, Potentiometers. Introduction to Strain gauges, Types of Strain Gauges,  Strain measuring technique, Requirements of  Strain Gauges,  Gauge factor, Alloys & materials, Surface preparation & bonding techniques.
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT V: ANGULAR VELOCITY & ACCELERATION MEASUREMENT
Introduction to Angular Velocity measurement, Mechanical tachometer, Electrical tachometer, Contactless electrical tachometers - Inductive pick-up tachometer, Capacitive pick-up tachometer, Photo-electric tachometer. Introduction to Acceleration measurement, Piezo-electric Accelerometer, Seismic accelerometer, Strain Gauge Accelerometer, LVDT Accelerometer, Potentiometric accelerometer.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: INDICATING & RECORDING MEANS

<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>
