<b>Experiments:</b>

<br><br><b>CHAIN SURVEYING</b>
<br>1.	Study of instruments required for chain surveying.
<br>2.	Ranging and chaining a line
<br>3.	Prolongation of  a survey line
<br>4.	Practice of reciprocal ranging
<br>5.	Practice of different methods of Setting out perpendiculars
<br>6.	Set out  Regular Polygons and compute its area
<br>7.	Practice of  cross staff survey
<br>8.	Conduct traversing and record the data in the Field book
<br>9.	Obstacles in chaining

<br><br><b>COMPASS SURVEYING</b>
<br>10.	Study of prismatic compass and surveyors compass
<br>11.	Taking bearings and finding the included angles by using prismatic compass
<br>12.	Set out  regular and irregular  Polygons  using prismatic compass
<br>13.	Find the distance between two inaccessible points using Compass

<br><br><b>LEVELLING</b>
<br>14.	Study of level and its  temporary adjustments
<br>15.	Taking level of various points and recording it in a level book
<br>16.	Finding the difference in elevation between  two points by  Simple and Differential Levelling
<br>17.	Conduct Fly leveling to establish a  Temporary BM and check its accuracy
<br>18.	Finding RL of given point by taking Inverted Staff Reading
<br>19.	Conduct Longitudinal and cross sectioning for the given alignment and plot it
<br>20.	Conduct Block Levelling for an area  and plot Contours
