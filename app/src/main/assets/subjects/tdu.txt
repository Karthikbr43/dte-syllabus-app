UNIT – I: ELECTRIC SUPPLY SYSTEMS
Definition, components, typical ac power supply scheme, Comparison of DC and AC transmission, Advantages and limitations of High transmission voltage (only list), various systems for power transmission.
<br>Elements of transmission line, factors for finding the economical transmission voltage, Requirements of satisfactory electric supply.

UNIT – II: TRANSMISSION MEDIUM
<b>OVERHEAD LINES</b>: Main components of overhead lines, Conductor materials- properties, commonly used materials, Line supports- properties, commonly used type, Insulators- properties and types, Corona- definition, formation, factors affecting corona, advantages and disadvantages, methods to reduce corona.
<br>Constants of a transmission line, Skin effect. 
<br>Classification of overhead transmission lines: voltage regulation and transmission efficiency, Short, medium, and long transmission lines- simple diagrams with explanation.
<br><b>UNDERGROUND CABLES</b>: Definition, necessary requirements, construction of a 3 conductor cable, Insulating materials for cables- properties, materials. Classification of cables, XLPE cables. Types of cable faults.
<br><br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT – III: HVDC, FACTS and SUBSTATIONS
<b>HVDC Transmission and FACTS</b>: Principle of HVDC system operation, advantages of HVDC transmission, Limitations of HVDC transmission, Comparison between EHV-AC and HVDC transmission, Types of DC links – monopolar DC link, bipolar DC link, Homopolar DC link. Ground return, earth electrode and station earth.
<br>FACTS Controllers- Definition, Objectives, functions of basic types of FACTS.
<br><b>SUBSTATIONS</b>: Function, points considered while laying out substation, classification, comparison between outdoor and indoor substation, symbols for equipment in substation,
<br><br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT – IV: POWER DISTRIBUTION
Definition, single line diagram of low tension distribution system, classification of distribution systems, AC distribution- classification. DC distribution- need and types, Overhead versus underground system, connection schemes of distribution system, requirements of a distribution system, design considerations in distribution system, Ground detectors. 
<br><b>Distribution Automation</b> - Need, characteristics, functions and benefits.
<br><b>SCADA</b> - Block diagram, functions and advantages of SCADA.
