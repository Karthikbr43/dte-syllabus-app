UNIT I: Introduction and Electrical current and  voltage
Introduction to sources of electrical energy, advantages of electrical energy and the effects of electric current and its applications.
<b>Electrical Current, Voltage and Circuits:</b> Electrical current, e.m.f., voltage, potential difference, open circuit, closed circuit and short circuit. Symbolic representation of sources, loads and basic protective devices.
<br><b>[05 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: DC  circuits and Resistance and temperature effects
<b>DC circuits:</b> Ohm’s law, Ohm’s law applications and its limitations. Simple problems on ohm’s law.
<br><b>Resistance and Temperature effects:</b>   Electrical Resistance and its unit.Series, Parallel and Series-Parallel circuits.Simple problems.
<br>Laws of Resistance, Specific Resistance and its unit. Effect of temperature on resistance and temperature co-efficient.Simple problems.
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [35 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: Work, Power and Energy
Work, Power, Energy, Problems on Power and energy, conversion from Mechanical units into Electrical units and vice- versa. Joule’s law of heat.Simple problems.
<br><b>[07 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: Electrostatics
Electric charge, Electric flux, Flux density, Electric field, Electric field intensity, Laws of electrostatics, dielectric constant and permittivity, Capacitance. Types of Capacitors, Capacitors in series and parallel, Energy stored in a capacitor. Simple problems on Electrostatic laws, capacitor combinations and energy stored.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT V: Faraday’s laws of Electrolysis
Laws, Difference between Cell and a Battery. Difference between Primary and Secondary Cells. Lead - Acid Battery- Construction, Chemical action during charging and discharging. Grouping of cells. Problems on grouping of cells.
<br><b>[09 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: Electrical Engineering Materials
Different Conductor materials, properties and applications. Different Resistor materials, properties and applications. Different Insulating materials, properties and applications. Different magnetic materials, properties and applications.
<br><b>[09 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>
