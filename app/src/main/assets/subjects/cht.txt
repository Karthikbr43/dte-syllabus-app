UNIT – I: ACID AND ALKALI INDUSTRIES
Manufacture of sulfur -sulfuric acid by contact process – Manufacture of nitric acid – Manufacture of phosphoric acid by wet process – Manufacture of hydrochloric acid –Manufacture of soda ash by Solvay process – Manufacture of chlorine and caustic soda by mercury cell and membrane cell process–– Manufacture of Bleaching powder and their industrial applications.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT – II: FERTILIZES AND PESTICIDES
Nitrogenous fertilizes – Manufacture of ammonia - manufacture of ammonium sulphate ,Ammonium chlorides, Ammonium nitrate – Ammonium phosphates – Manufacture of Urea by total recycle process—Manufacture of NPK fertilizers –Pesticides –  – Manufacture of DDT, BHC, 2,4D, parathion. 
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT - III: GLASS AND CEMENT TECHNOLOGY
Types of glasses – Composition – Raw material – Commercial applications, manufacture of plate glass – Coloured and safety glass—Raw material for cements, types of cements – Manufacture of cements by wet and dry process .
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - IV: OILS, FATS AND DETERGENTS
Chemical composition and physical properties of edible oils, distinction between oils and fats - Definition of acid value saponification value – Iodine value – Mechanical expression of oil - Solvent extraction of vegetable  oil – Hydrogenation of oil – Definition of detergents and soaps – Manufactures of soaps by full boiled process – Recovery of glycerin from spent lye – Manufacture of linear alkyl benzene – ABS-Surf.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT - V: PULP AND PAPER,SUGAR AND FERMENTATION TECHNOLOGY
Raw materials for paper, pulp -production of pulp by  kraft or sulphate process– Chemical recovery from spent cooking liquor in kraft Process – Sulphite process (magnetite process) – Semi chemical pulping –  Preparation of pulp  for paper production -Production of paper from pulp. Composition of sugarcane and cane juice – Terms used in sugar industry – (Brix -  Polarity – Purity – Massecuite – Molasses - Bagasse) – Manufacture of cane sugar – refining of sugar.Terms used in fermentation process – Manufacture of ethyl alcohol – Citric acid – Vinegar Gelatin – Their uses. ––Manufacture of starch from maize .
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT – VI: PETROCHEMICAL AND POLYMER TECHNOLOGY
Manufacture of   methanol - formaldehyde – ethylene – acetylene - vinyl chloride –ethylene oxide –– acetone –butadiene  – Styrene - phenol and their applications.
<br>Definition of polymer – Classification reactions involved in the formation of polymers – Methods of polymerization – Manufacture of poly ethylene by Ziegler process – Manufacture of polyvinylchloride– Manufacture of polystyrene, phenol formaldehyde  - Polyester –Natural rubber, manufacture of synthetic rubber.( SBR )
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>
