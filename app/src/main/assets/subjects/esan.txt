UNIT - I: COMMUNICABLE DISEASES
<b>Introduction:</b> Definition of health and sanitation, Importance of sanitation-Activities involved in Sanitation, Public health works.
<br><b>Communicable diseases:</b> Definitions- epidemic, endemic, epizootic, channel of infection, Vehicle of infection, Incubation period, Disease communicated by intestinal discharge. Typhoid fever transmission & prevention. Hook worm disease transmission & prevention, Disease communicated by nose and throat discharges. Tuberculosis –Mode of transmission & its control. Disease of animals transmitted to the human beings-Anthrax, Rabies and Brucellosis, General method of control of communicable diseases. Prevention of epidemics.
<br><br><b>[09 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT - II: FOOD SANITATION, INSECTS VECTORS AND RODENT CONTROL
<b>Food sanitation:</b> Importance of food sanitation, Agencies through which food may cause suffering or death. Food & drug law, Food borne diseases; Food poisoning. Streptococcus intoxication and botolinous intoxication, Storage & protection of food in restaurant, Milk borne diseases, Essentials of milk sanitation. Health and habits of dairy workman, dairy barn, milk house, pasteurization of milk
<br><b>Insect vector & Rodent control:</b> Role of mosquito as a carrier of diseases with examples, life cycle of mosquito and identification of mosquito, Larvicidal methods of control of mosquitoes, Diseases spread by flies, measures adopted to control the flies, Species of Rodent, rodent as an economical factor, diseases spread through rodents - Plague, Typhus fever Anti rodent measures 
<br><br><b>[14 Hrs] [40 Marks] </b>

UNIT - III: EXCERETA DISPOSAL IN UNSEWRERED AREA
<b>Excreta disposal in unsewered areas:</b> Importance, & requirements of sanitary latrine, Pit privy, Bore hole privy, Aqua privy, Concrete vault privy. 
<br><b>Waste water treatment in unsewered areas:</b> Construction & working of septic tank. Design criteria and design of septic tank.  Methods of disposal of septic tank effluents.
<br><br><b>[06 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - IV: REFUSE AND INSTITUTIONAL SANITATION
<b>Refuse sanitation:</b> Composition and sources of solid waste, Storage and separation of solid waste, Collection of solid waste; Solid waste disposal - sanitary land filling, incineration and composting.
<br><b>Institutional sanitation:</b> Importance of school sanitation, School health programme, Selection site for School building, furnishing for school  
<br><br><b>[10 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT - V: LIGHTING AND VENTILATION
Advantages of good lighting, recommended minimum level of illumination for different tasks, Bunsen’s bar photometer for measurement of light, Glare, removal of Shadow. Objects of ventilation & characteristics of good ventilation, Types of ventilation and air conditioning.
<br><br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT - VI: OCCUPATIONAL HAZARDS
Occupational hazards from various sources, General methods for protection of workers from occupational hazards, Threshold limit value , Disease caused due to inhalation of dust & their control, Disease caused due to industrial poisons, Light, heat, compressed air as occupational hazard
<br><br><b>[07 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>
