UNIT – I : PRINCIPLES OF MASS TRANSFER
Principles of diffusion in fluids and solids – Molecular diffusion – Fick’s law of diffusion -Eddy   diffusion. Classification of mass transfer operations – Methods of conducting mass transfer operation – Unsteady state and steady state operations - Stage wise operations – Differential contact operations. 
<br><b>[07 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT – II: HUMIDIFICATION
Humidification operations – Vapor liquid equilibrium and enthalpy of  a pure substance – Definition of humidification terms – Absolute and molal humidity – Dry and wet bulb temperature – Relative humidity – percentage humidity dew point – Humid volume – Humid heat – Enthalpy – Humidity chart – Construction and use – Adiabatic saturation temperature – Lewi’s  relation – Cooling towers – Humidification and dehumidification towers.
<br><b>[07 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT - III: VAPOR LIQUID EQUILIBRIUM IN DISTILLATION
Binary and multi  component systems – Vapor liquid equilibrium at constant pressure and constant temperature – Raoult’s law – Maximum and minimum boiling azeotropes – Enthalpy – concentration diagram – Application of Raoult’s law to predict vapor liquid equilibrium and problem in application of Raoult’s law.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - IV :  METHODS OF DISTILLATION
Methods of distillation – Batch distillation – Ray Leigh’s equation – problems in ray Leigh’s- equations – Flash distillation – Binary continuous rectification – Tray towers – Calculation of number of trays – MC Cabe Thiele method-assumptions in MC cabe Thiele method – Graphical procedure to determine the number of theoretical trays – Location of feed plate – q– line and operating lines – Total reflux – Minimum reflux – Optimum reflux – Tray efficiency.
<br>Molecular distillation – Azeotropic distillation – Extractive distillation – Steam distillation – Principles and applications – Brief discussion about multi component distillation and use of packed towers for distillation – Tray towers construction – Operation and problems during operation.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT – V : CRYSTALLIZATION
Principles of crystallization – Solubility – Mechanism of crystallization – Nucleation and crystal growth –crystal forms- crystal habit- Miers supersaturation theory-Rate of crystal growth-caking of crystals- crystal humidity- prevention of caking- Classification of crystallizer- tank crystallizer- Agitated batch crystallizer – Swenson – Walker crystallizer – Vacuum crystallizer –simple vacuum crystallizer- circulating magma crystallizer- Krystal crystallizer-Evaporative crystallizer - Description and operation of the above equipments. 
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - VI: DRYING
Principles – Equilibrium – Definition of terms – Wet basis moisture content –Dry basis moisture content – Equilibrium moisture – Bound moisture - Unbound moisture and free moisture – Batch drying – Drying characteristics - Constant rate drying – Falling rate drying - Time of drying – Mechanism of moisture movement within the solid – Diffusion mechanism - Capillary mechanism - Drying equipments – Batch driers – Tray drier – Continuous drier -Rotary drier - – Drum drier – Spray drier – Tunnel drier - Equipments description and applications.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>
