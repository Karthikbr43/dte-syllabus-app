<b>Experiments:</b>

<br><br><b>PART - A: MECHANICAL  TESTING LAB</b>

<br><br>1.	Determine co-efficient of friction of any two oil by using Thurston oil tester and compare their results.
<br>2.	Determine flash and fire point of any two lubricants of different grade and compare their results
<br>3.	Calculate Impact Value of Mild Steel & Copper using CHARPY Impact Test & compare their results.
<br>4.	Calculate Impact Value of Mild Steel using IZOD Impact Test Apparatus 
<br>5.	Calculate Brinell Hardness Number of given material 
<br>6.	Calculate Hardness of given material using Rockwell Hardness machine 
<br>7.	Calculate Hardness of given material using Vickers Hardness machine 
<br>8.	Determination of yield stress, ultimate stress, percentage reduction in area, percentage elongation, Youngâ€™s modulus by conducting tension test on Ductile Materials like Mild Steel, Aluminium in Universal testing machine. Draw Stress Strain Curve for both and compare their results.
<br>9.	Find out Compressive Strength of C.I , M.S using Compression Testing Machine
<br>10.	Conducting bending test on wood specimen by UTM and evaluate the results

<br><br><b>PART - A: FINISHING TECHNIQUE</b>
<br><br>1.	Demonstration of equipments and process in Electroplating shop.
<br>2.	Determine the effects of Hydrochloric acid, Sulphuric acid and Nitric acid on different metals.
<br>3.	Practice of dressing wheels. Practice the process of polishing and buffing of different metals. Practice of chemical cleaning on mild steel and other metals.
<br>4.	Demonstration of process used for sample preparation for plating such as Grinding & Sand blasting. Demonstration of process in barred finishing scratch brushing.
<br>5.	Preparing various electrolytes for electroplating of copper, Nickel and Chromium. Testing the solution on different metals to determination of PH value for the above solutions.
<br>6.	Preparing the various metals for electroplating of Zinc plating on mild steel & colouring. Preparing the various metals for electroplating of Copper plating on mild steel.
<br>7.	Preparing the various metals for electroplating of nickel and Chrome plating on Brass. Preparing the various metals for electroplating of nickel and chrome plating on Copper.
<br>8.	Anodizing on a given Aluminium articles. Colouring on anodized Aluminium articles.
<br>9.	Practice of Etching & Embossing on Aluminium and Brass.
