UNIT I: INTRODUCTION 
Definition and objectives of surveying, primary divisions, classifications, and principle.	
<br>CHAIN SURVEYING : Purpose, Accessories, Principles of chain surveying, Different operations, Ranging, Cross staff survey simple problems, Plotting the chain survey, adopting suitable conventions, Errors and  corrections in chain surveying, (without numerical problems)
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [28 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: COMPASS SURVEYING	
Introduction and purpose, Bearing and its type, Problems on bearings, Compass and its type, Dip and declination, Simple problems, Local attraction, Open and closed traverse, checks, Errors		
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [28 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: LEVELLING
Terms used in leveling, types of levels, Bench marks, Temporary adjustments of level	Concept of B.S, I.S, F.S, C.P, H.I and remarks, Simple leveling and differential leveling Reduction of levels i) Plane of collimation method ii) Rise and fall methods
<br>Problems on reduction of levels.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [28 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: LEVELLING – APPLICATION
Different types of leveling - fly leveling, check leveling, profile leveling, cross sectioning,  Plotting of longitudinal and cross section, Errors in leveling and precautions, Setting grade stakes and setting out grades for sewers and problems on it.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [28 Marks] </b>

UNIT V: CONTOURING   
Concepts of contour and terms used in contouring, characteristics of contour, uses of contours, Methods of contouring, Interpolation by arithmetical method, calculation of capacity of the reservoir. 
<br><b>[05 Hrs] [19 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: AREAS AND VOLUMES
Computation of Area of Irregular figures using Trapezoidal and   Simpson's rule - problems.   Volumes of Irregular solids- using Trapezoidal and Prismoidal Rule - Problems on Embankment and Cutting 
<br><b>[07 Hrs] [14 Marks] </b>
