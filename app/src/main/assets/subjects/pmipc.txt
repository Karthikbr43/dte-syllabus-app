UNIT – I: PLANT MAINTENANCE
Need for maintenance-feature of break down-schedules-corrective, preventive and productive maintenance-procedure effort of maintenance on output and quality-maintenance of schedules-cost of maintenance-co-ordinate of maintenance department with other-spare part control-equipment replacement analysis. Preparing equipment for maintenance-vessel entry-lubrication-types of lubricant-purpose of lubrication-properties of lubricant-maintenance of equipment on stream-fittings, pipes and lines, valves, boilers, heat exchangers-high pressure vessels, batch reactor, air compressors, centrifugal pumps.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT – II: HAZARDS MANAGEMENT
Detection- methods to detect- types of hazard-hazard analysis & management –HAZOP Analysis- PHA-check list-risk management& measures-chemical hazard -dust –thermal- electric. Hazard assessment.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT – III: WATER  PURIFICATION
Water sources-importance of water-Waste water characteristic-Water test-Hardness-PH –COD-BOD-TDS-TOD-TOC-Thod-Water purification-Settling-Sedimentation-Filtration-Lime soda-Ionic exchange process-Zeolite - Reverse osmosis process-Deminerization of water-Desalination-Boiler feed water treatment process.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT – IV: EFFLUENT TREATMENT
Various methods of waste water treatment- screening- settling- flocculation-filtration-coagulation-trickling filters-Activated sludge process-sludge digestion-lagooning-oxidation pond-land treatment.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT – V: AIR & NOISE POLLUTION AND CONTROL
Sources-Effects-Classification of pollutant-Methods of control-Particulate control-Control at source & Process changes-Equipment of settling chamber-Cyclone-Fabric filter-Electrostatic –Wet scrubber-Adsorption Equipment. Noise pollution: Sources of noise-Type of industrial noise & that’s effect- Reduction of noise
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT – VI: INDUSTRIAL WASTE WATER TREATMENT
Sugar  industry - pulp & paper - tanning – cement - breweries – pesticides- fertilizer- refinery.
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>
