UNIT - I: ORIGIN-DRILLING-COMPOSITION AND PROPERTIES
Origin and formation of petroleum –  Occurrence of petroleum- Theories of origin –Indian resources – Indian petroleum industries – Composition of  crude petroleum –– Exploration and production-  Drilling of petroleum – Transportation – Storage.  Important products – properties of petroleum products – Specific gravity – Viscosity – Distillation range – flash and fire point – pour point and cloud point – smoke point and char point – carbon residue – sulphur and moisture content – calorific value – octane number – cetane number – Aniline point- Environmental concerns & innovative methods to reduce energy consumption, for recovery & reuse of valuable materials in the above. 
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [40 Marks] </b>
 
UNIT – II: FRACTONATION AND TREATMENT TECHNIQUES
Dehydration and desalting – chemical treatment, settling and electric desalting - Heating of crude – Pipe still heaters and furnaces Distillation of petroleum methods –  Arrangements of towers – Vacuum distillation unit and stages wise distillation units -  Production from distillation – Blending operation – integrated refineries.
<br>Treatment  techniques :  Fractions and impurities – Sweetening process – Production and treatment of LPG – absorption techniques – gas liquefaction – Amine treatment -   gasoline  Treatment – by copper chloride process – dualayer process – lead doctoring of gasoline – merox  process – sulphuric acid  treatment  - catalytic desulphurization – Treatment Kerosene – liquid sulphur dioxide extraction – Treatment of  lube oils – Clay treatment – solvent refining  – phenol extraction – furfural extraction – duosol process  - Wax and purification – dewaxing  process sweating and solvent dewaxing ( MEK  & propane dewaxing.)- Environmental concerns & innovative methods to reduce energy consumption, for recovery & reuse of valuable materials like solvents, byproducts, acid sludge etc., in the above.
<br><b>[20 Hrs] [50 Marks] </b>

UNIT – III: THERMAL AND CATALYTIC PROCESSES
Cracking – Thermal cracking reactions – Cracked products and properties – Cracking operation – Catalytic cracking – Reactions – Catalysts used – Advantages of Catalytic cracking – Fixed bed ( Houdry  process) – Moving bed : Air lift -  Thermo for Catalytic cracking  process – and Houdry flow process – Fluidized bed processes : Fluid Catalytic cracking  process – Flexi and Ortho flow Catalytic cracking - Catalytic reforming – naphtha cracking  Coking – delayed and fluid coking -  flexi coking – continuous contact coking – Hydrogen processes : Hydro cracking -  Isomax , HG processes – Moving bed  hydro crackers – Hydro fining  operation. –visbreaking- 
<br>Environmental concerns & innovative methods to reduce energy consumption, for recovery & reuse of valuable materials like catalysts, solvents, byproducts of coking, gaseous products in the above.
<br><b>[20 Hrs] [55 Marks] </b>
