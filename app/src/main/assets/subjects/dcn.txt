UNIT - I: Introduction to data communication and networks
<b>Data communication</b>-data representation, data flow, components. Definition of node, link, branch, network, network criteria. <b>Physical structures</b>-types of connection, working of different network topologies, network configuration and their advantages, concepts and comparison of LAN, MAN, WAN. Switching - concepts of circuit switching, packet switching and message switching and their applications.  
<br><b>[07 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - II: Networking protocols and OSI model
<b>Protocol layering</b>-Scenarios, principles. Logical connection-connection oriented and connection less. Protocols in computer communications, <b>OSI reference model</b> - functions of all layers. <b>Data link control</b>- concept of framing, flow control and error control. <b>MAC protocol</b>- addressing mechanism. Concept of encapsulation and decapsulation.
<br><b>[07 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - III: Computer Networks
<b>Local area network</b>-wired LANs features and classification. <b>Ethernet</b>- properties, frame format (IEEE 802.3), addressing, simple problems on addressing. <b>Virtual LAN</b>- working, advantages. <b>Access method</b>–CSMA/CD. <b>Token passing LANS</b>- properties, token bus maintenance and working. Token ring properties, mechanism. <b>FDDI</b> –operation, self healing, <b>Wireless LANS</b> - features, Bluetooth architecture (IEEE 802.15). Basic concepts of WIMAX, cellular telephony, satellite networks. 
<br><b>[11 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - IV: TCP/IP
<b>TCP/IP</b>-Model, protocols layers, INTERNET Address, logical address, Physical address, UDP/IP Datagram Format, classes of IP address, Dotted Decimal notation of IP address,  basics of IPv4 and IPv6,simple problems on addressing. <b>Address mapping</b> –static mapping, dynamic mapping. <b>ARP</b>- need, methods, need of RARP and ICMP. Definition of fragmentation and reassembly. Features of TCP, relationship between TCP and IP. 
<br><b>[11 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - V: Communication protocols
Concepts of Ports and Sockets. <b>Domain Name System (DNS)</b> -name system, name space, working of DNS server. <b>Email</b>- architecture, protocols, advantages of IMAP. Basics of FTP, <b>FTP Connections</b> - Control and Data transfer Connection. <b>Frame relay</b>- Need, Working of frame relay, <b>ATM</b>- Architecture, characteristics.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - VI: Internet devices and protocols
<b>Internetworking</b>-need and concept. <b>Connecting Devices</b>-discussion on Routers, switches, repeaters, Bridges, Switches and Gateways. <b>Ways of accessing the internet</b>- Dial-up access, SLIP, PPP, leased lines, DSL basics, internet access by cable. <b>Modems</b>-basics, types, operation, applications. <b>Network security</b>-basics of threats and fire wall.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>
