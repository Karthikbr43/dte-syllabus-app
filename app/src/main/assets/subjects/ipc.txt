UNIT I: Process variables & temperature, pressure measuring instruments
Process variable – static and dynamic characteristics of process instruments – measurement of temperature-different types of thermometers – bimetallic thermometer, industrialthermometer,mercury-in-glassthermometer,resistance ,thermocouples,radiation pyrometer,optical pyrometer- principles involved – working. Instruments used for pressure measurement – bourdon gauge – Bellows-Diaphragms – Vacuum measuring instruments-pirani gauge
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: Measuring instruments- Level, Viscosity, Humidity, Ph, Density
Direct level measurement-Bob and tape, Sight or gauge   glass method,Float and tape guage,float and shaft method.Indirect level measurement –air or gas purge method, pressuregauge method, flowmeasurment-orificemeter,venturimeter,pitot tube, rotameter, humidity measurement- psychrometric method, Dew point method, density- hygrometer,viscosity-redwood viscometer.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: Process Control
Principles and uses of automatic process control, block diagram,physical diagram,operational diagram – Modes of control – Principles of ON/OFF – Proportional – Derivative – Integral, PI ,PD, PID  and P-I-D control	,Response-open loop and closed loop
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: Controllers
Working principles of pneumatic controllers – proportional,derivative,integral ,proportional and integral, proportional  and derivative, proportional,integral and derivative controllers, Electrical –P-controller,PI,PD,PID, Electronic controllers –  P-controler,PI,PD,PID,Role  of micro processors in process control,Digital control systems.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT V:Final control elements
Final control elements- introduction – elements of control valves – sliding stem valves,rotary stem valves, valve plug designs,actuators, Pneumatic – Mechanical - Electrical types - Working principles.-  Application of Computers in process control
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: Analytical instruments
Principles of chromatography – Gas liquid chromatography – Thermal analysis – Principles – Working of DTA – TGA (Thermo Gravimetric Analyser) - Spectrometers – Principles - Working of U.V. Spectrometers – INFRA Red spectrometer
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>
