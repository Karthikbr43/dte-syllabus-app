UNIT I: INTRODUCTION TO ENGINEERING DRAWING AND LETTERING PRACTICE
Drawing Instruments
<br>Standard Sizes of Drawing sheets-Layout of drawing sheets
<br>Types of lines and their applications
<br>Different types of lettering as per I.S.I; 
<br>Uppercase  letters of vertical and slanting type as  per I.S.I 
<br>Numerical figures of vertical and slanting type as per I.S.I.
<br><b>[09 Hrs] [18 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: DIMENSIONING PRACTICE
Introduction to Dimensioning, Elements of Dimensioning, Systems of Dimensioning, Methods of arrangements of Dimensioning
<br>Dimensioning of common features like diameters, radii, arcs and chords. Dimensioning of simple civil Engineering Objects.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [11 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: GEOMETRICAL CONSTRUCTIONS AND CONIC SECTIONS   
Drawing of tangents to circles and arcs, Drawing a common tangent of given arcs to circles of equal or unequal radii.
<br>Inscribing a circle in a regular polygon -Inscribing circles touching each side of a regular polygon and its two adjacent circles. 
<br>Inscribing circles touching two sides of polygon and two other circles - graded exercises.				
<br>Division of a line into equal number of parts 
<br>Introduction to conic sections-Types of conic section  
<br>Construction of ellipse by Intersecting lines method (Rectangular and parallelogram methods) and Concentric circles method 
<br>Construction of parabola by rectangle method, parallelogram method and tangential method.
<br><b>[15 Hrs] [29 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: SCALES
Construction of plain and diagonal scales and marking distances on scales constructed - exercises
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [12 Marks] </b>

UNIT V: ORTHOGRAPHIC PROJECTIONAND PROJECTION OF POINTS
Introduction to orthographic projection-Principal planes of projection- Four Quadrants- Concept of First angle and Third angle projection methods- Projection of points in all the four quadrant system.
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [22 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: PROJECTION OF LINES AND  PLANES SURFACES  (Only first angle projection)
Projection of lines – Line Parallel to both HP and VP, Line parallel to one plane and Perpendicular to other-Line parallel to one plane and Inclined to the other, Line inclined to both HP and VP. 
<br>Projection of plane Surfaces -Construction of polygons
<br>Plane surface parallel to one plane and Perpendicular to other two – Plane surface Perpendicular to one plane and inclined to the other- Plane surface  inclined to both HP and VP
<br><b>[30 Hrs] [58 Marks] </b>
