UNIT I: Introduction to sheet metal technology, tools and machinery
Introduction, job categories, Fundamentals of sheet metal work, Hand tools –scratch awls, dividers, steel square, trammel points, rule. Punches-prick, centre, solid, hollow, hand lever. Hand Grover, Rivet set, Chisels-Flat, cape, diamond point, round nose. Hammers and types. Mallet, Snips, Pliers, Handy seamer or tongs, soldering coppers, Hacksaws, files, Bench stakes and stake holder, machines and supports, Burring, turning, Wiring, Creeping, Beading , rotary circular shears, hand brake, Pan  brake, press brake, power shear.
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [40 Marks] </b>

UNIT II: Sheet metal types and operations
Steel sheet metal-coated and solid sheets, sheet steel gages, measuring tools, Galvanized sheet     metal, stainless steel, Tin plate, Non-ferrous like copper, aluminium, lead, zinc, oxide of metals.
<br>Cutting operation-producing blanks, cutting holes, progressive, miscellaneous operation. Forming operation-bending, flanging, hemming, seaming, curling, wiring, ribbing, staking, crimping, bulging, beading, enclosing, tube forming. Drawing operation-- cupping, box drawing, panel drawing, Shallow, deep panel drawing.
<br><b>[12 Hrs] [35 Marks] </b>

UNIT III: Materials and Fasteners for Sheet Metal
Mild steel shapes, materials for opening, pipe and tubing, wire and dampers, Fasteners-rivets, bolts and machine screws, sheet metal screws
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>

UNIT IV: Sheet metal patterns
Introduction, Methods of developments with example like, developing patterns for rectangular duct, round pipe, rectangular tray, cone.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>
 
UNIT V: Pneumatic and Hydraulic tools
Pneumatic tools for riveting, drilling, hammering,  Hydraulic pipe bending machine,  Cone rollers and corrugated curved rollers,  Circular shearing
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT VI: Safety recommendations in Sheet Metal Shop
Positive attitude, General safety, Squaring shears, Brakes, soldering, sheet metal, welding, power tools, noise control, housekeeping.
<br><b>[06 Hrs] [15 Marks] </b>
