UNIT - I: Terracotta
Definition, describe the classification (porous and polished terracotta), properties and field of application. Explain the manufacturing of roofing tile and wire cut bricks with a neat flow chart. Describe the important tests on terracotta like ccs. List possible defects ( black coring, bloating, exploding, fracturing, warping, voids cracking)   in terracotta with the causes and remedies.
<br><b>[09 Hrs] [25 Marks] </b>

UNIT - II: Earthen ware
Definition, classification of earthenware with examples. Write the composition of different earthenwares. Definition of engobe, need of engobe, specify the required qualities of engobe and typical compositions of enboge. Illustrate the recent manufacturing process and advances in wall & floor tiles, difference between wall and floor tiles. Describe various defects and their remedies in different stages of manufacturing (drying – surface cracks, warping, laminations & firing defects – stuck ware, over firing, sulphuring). Discuss the important tests like crazing resistance, colour match. 
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - III: Plaster of Paris
Describe POP, types and its properties. Illustrate manufacturing process of POP from Gypsum. Explain the calcinations of gypsum in detail. Uses of Plaster Of Paris in general and in ceramic industries. Describe the importance of water plaster ratio in mould preparation, list the properties of POP mould, tests on POP – sieve test, IST, FST and porosity and absorption for POP moulds. Making of casting mould.
<br><b>[08 Hrs] [20 Marks] </b>

UNIT - IV: Stone ware
Definition, write the classification with examples, give the compositions of different stonewares. Explain the recent manufacturing methods (pressure, vacuum, centrifugal casting) of different fine sanitary wares and extrusion of stoneware pipes with neat flow diagrams. Describe glazing of stoneware pipes by vapour/salt glazing method. Identify the Common defects in stoneware, mention their causes with remedies. Describe tests on stone wares like hydraulic pressure, Absorption, chemical resistance and thermal shock test.
<br><b>[10 Hrs] [30 Marks] </b>

UNIT - V: Porcelain
Definition, classification (soft paste porcelain and hard paste porcelain) with examples. Write the compositions for Bone china wares, its manufacturing (single firing and double firing) with flow diagram, properties and applications. Describe low tension and high tension porcelain, technical datasheet and applications. Explain the manufacturing of low tension insulator and high tension insulators (disc and bushings) with neat flow diagram. Describe the turning process of disc insulators and bushings. Discuss their physical, mechanical, and electrical properties and common tests conducted on insulators. Discuss the rutile and steatite bodies and produce a comparative statement between them. Define spark plug; explain its manufacturing with flow chart and properties.
<br><b>[15 Hrs] [40 Marks] </b>
